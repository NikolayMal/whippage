Whip FAQ's

How do I register?

Select the "account." dropdown menu on the rop right of your device and enter the page. Then you will be prompted to enter your details to create your account with Whippage Cars.

How do I purchase a vehicle?

Navigate towards the cars page and choose the vehicle that you want to purchase. Click the "purchase' button below the image and it will navigate you through the next steps that you would have to take to complete your purchase.

How do will I get my vehicle that I purchased?

Your vehicle will be shipped out of our warehouse in Bol'shoi Lug, Irkutsk. It will be brought to you 2weeks* from the date of purchase.

What methods of payment do I have?

We accept many forms of payments ranging from Visa Debit Cards, Paypal and cryptocurrency.

Am I able to request a car if I don't see it in the cars page?

Yes! We take car requests from our customers seriously and if we do not have the car you want in stock, we will work our magic and find the car that is perfect for you.

I have a car that I would like to sell to Whippage Cars, how can I do this?

The easiest way to do this is to get in contact with us! We will walk through the steps and if the car is worthy of being on Whipapge Cars, we will purchase it from you.
